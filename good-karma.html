#QuarterlyInspection

The Quarterly Inspection

Third-party verification every 3 months. No he-said-she-said. Just facts, photos, and scores.

1

Schedule

Manay schedules the inspection. Both landlord and tenant get 7 days' notice. No surprises.

2

Inspect

Certified inspector visits for 30-45 minutes. 8 categories scored. Photos documented. Both parties can attend.

3

Report

Digital report delivered to both landlord and tenant. Disputes can be raised within 7 days. Otherwise, scores stand.

4

Karma Update

Inspection scores feed directly into your Karma. Good upkeep = higher score = better perks. It's that simple.
