Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Manay.

What are Zero-Chase Payments?

Zero-Chase Payments means you never have to chase tenants for rent again. Rent is automatically deducted via UPI Mandate on the due date. We handle reminders, follow-ups, and late payment notifications. You just receive the deposit — zero chasing required.

Is the Digital Agreement included in my subscription?

No. The Digital Agreement is a separate one-time purchase. It covers e-stamping, Aadhaar e-sign, and registration-ready documentation — valid for 1 year. Your subscription (whether landlord or tenant plan) does not include this.

Is Manay compliant with the 2026 Karnataka rental laws?

Yes. Manay is built around the January 2026 Karnataka Rent (Amendment) Bill. We help you stay compliant with the 2-month deposit cap, handle Kaveri 2.0 registration within 60 days, and provide documentation for fast-track Rent Tribunals.

Where is Manay launching first?

We're launching first in South and Central Bengaluru. Other areas in Bengaluru and major Karnataka cities will follow. Join the waitlist to be notified when we're in your area.

How does credit score building work for tenants?

We report your on-time rent payments to credit bureaus. Paying rent on time each month improves your credit score — making it easier to get home loans, credit cards, and better interest rates in the future.
